THE JOURNEY

From Lenapehoking to the United Nations
01
2018 – 2022

The Foundation

Technology is powerful. It is also, for most people, invisible — and that invisibility is not an accident. I started in IT because I wanted to understand how systems actually work. But the deeper question was always: who gets left out when they don't? Data literacy, environmental sensing, GIS — these aren't just technical skills. They are tools for helping people understand what is happening in their own neighborhoods, their own air, their own world. Technology should not be something that happens to communities. It should be something communities can read, question, and use.
